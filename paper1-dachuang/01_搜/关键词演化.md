```

  ↓ 第一轮精炼：加入干燥、风味、挥发物限定

  ↓ 第二轮扩展：发现 GC-IMS/GC-MS/LC-MS 为核心技术词

  ↓ 第四轮方法论对标：发现 Nat Commun 2024 啤酒风味 ML、Trends Food Sci 2026 Digital Twin
"multi-omics machine learning flavor prediction food"
  ↓ 确认最终检索式，进入【聚→分】
```

## 最终检索式（进入【聚→分】的版本）

### 核心检索式（英文）
```
(Morchella OR "morel mushroom") 
AND (drying OR dehydration OR "hot-air drying" OR "freeze drying") 
AND (flavor OR volatile OR aroma OR metabolomics OR "GC-MS" OR "GC-IMS" OR "LC-MS")
```

### 方法对标检索式
```
("machine learning" OR "deep learning" OR "neural network" OR "random forest" OR XGBoost) 
AND ("flavor prediction" OR "aroma prediction" OR "quality prediction") 
AND (food OR mushroom OR "edible fungi")
```
